About Us

Explore Auctum Solutions

Auctum Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a premier contract manufacturing company based in Karnataka, India. With decades of experience, we specialize in creating high-quality Home Care and Divine Care products that resonate with trust and purity.

Our infrastructure spans across two strategic locations—Bengaluru and Mandya—equipped with modern machinery for mixing, filling, sealing, and packaging. We pride ourselves on our ability to handle large-scale production while maintaining the flexibility to cater to niche market requirements.

We serve as the backbone for numerous established brands, delivering excellence from raw material sourcing to final packaging, ensuring your customers receive nothing but the best.
